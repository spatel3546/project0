Indian Student Association

At Mountain Ridge High School, in my sophomore year, I founded the Indian Student Association, or ISA. The focus of this club was to bring Mountain Ridge's Indian student body together to celebrate our culture and identity. In ISA, we hold Bollywood movie nights, Garba events, food tastings, and Indian clothing drives. Below is a table of our most popular events for 2018, 2019, and 2020:



2018 2019 2020
Gujarati Food Tasting Garba Workshop Bollywood Night at Harkins
