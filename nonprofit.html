My Non-Profit: Tech for Tails





Tech for Tails is a non-profit organization I founded in March of 2020. The purpose of this non-profit is to provide for the underfunded kennels and animal shelters in the grater Phoenix area. At Tech for Tails, we host short coding workshops and provide website services to small businesses and organizations in exchange for a small donation, all of which goes to animal shelters in Arizona. So far, we have collected about $2000.
